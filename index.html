Questions

Frequently asked.

Who is Mohammed Rishab?

A full-stack developer and an engineer based in India. I build production applications across web, AI, and embedded systems — from React frontends to Hardware projects. My work focuses on solving real-world problems with pragmatic engineering.

What does “full-stack” mean in your context?

Frontend (React, Next.js, TypeScript), backend (Node.js, FastAPI, PostgreSQL, Docker, AWS), AI integration (LLMs, RAG, LangChain), and IoT / embedded (ESP32, Arduino, MQTT). I don’t just write code — I design systems end to end.

What kind of projects do you build?

Everything I build starts with a real problem. ImpulseQ tackles hospital queue chaos. GrantPanda helps students find scholarships they’d otherwise miss. Bus In Time brings GPS tracking to public transit. I pick problems that affect real people.

Are you available for freelance or full-time roles?

Yes. I’m open to freelance projects, contract work, and full-time engineering roles. If you’re building something that solves a genuine problem, I’d love to hear about it. Reach me at contactmrishab@gmail.com.
